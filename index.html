Hello, I'm Mehmet Öndüç. I hold a bachelor's degree in Visual Communication Design and have completed my master's studies in the same field, where I sharpened my ability to combine aesthetics with functionality. My journey into software development began with a deep fascination for coding, which brings me a sense of clarity and tranquility as I transform ideas into tangible solutions. Writing code is more than just a skill for me; it is a creative outlet that inspires me to innovate and solve complex problems. I am passionate about bridging the worlds of design and technology, and I believe that collaboration and professional ethics are key to achieving meaningful outcomes. My GitHub portfolio showcases projects that integrate visual design principles with cutting-edge programming techniques, reflecting my dedication to continuous learning and impactful teamwork.
